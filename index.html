Poultry Schemes

Government subsidies and financial aid programs are essential for modernizing poultry infrastructure.

Central Govt Schemes

National Livestock Mission (NLM)

Under its “Entrepreneurship Development & Employment Generation (EDEG)” component, NLM supports establishment of rural poultry farms via capital subsidies. Subsidy available up to a certain ceiling ( ₹ 25 lakh for poultry projects).

Poultry Venture Capital Fund (PVCF)

NABARD acts as a channelising agency under NLM for subsidies & financial assistance. Their “Poultry Layer Farming” guidebook outlines how layer farming can be a viable income source and gives guidance on farm size, economics etc.

Animal Husbandry Infrastructure (AHIDF)

Aimed at boosting investment in animal-husbandry infrastructure. Poultry farmers / entrepreneurs can use this fund to get financial assistance.

Kisan Credit Card (KCC)

There is a scheme offering up to 50% subsidy (up to a defined limit) for setting up poultry farms. This helps reduce initial capital outlay.
